Sushi

Fresh & Exquisite Sushi Creations

At Food, LLC, our sushi is more than just a meal—it's an art form. Made with the freshest seafood and premium ingredients, each roll is handcrafted with precision, balance, and beauty. Whether you're a sushi enthusiast or a curious first-timer, our sushi menu offers something for every palate.

Customer Favorites

  • California Roll – A classic favorite made with crab meat, avocado, and cucumber, wrapped in seaweed and rice, topped with sesame seeds.

  • Salmon Nigiri – Thinly sliced fresh salmon delicately placed over seasoned rice, served with wasabi and soy sauce.

  • Tempura Shrimp Roll – Crispy shrimp tempura paired with creamy avocado and drizzled with spicy mayo and soy glaze.

Made Fresh to Order

Our sushi chefs take pride in every roll, ensuring freshness, balance, and authenticity in every bite. From traditional nigiri to inventive fusion rolls, we blend Japanese technique with bold flavors that will keep you coming back for more.
